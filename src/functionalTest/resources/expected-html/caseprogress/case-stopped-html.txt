<h2 class="govuk-heading-l">What happens next</h2>
<h2 class="govuk-heading-l">Beth fydd yn digwydd nesaf</h2>
<p class="govuk-body-s">The case was stopped on 26 Sept 2024 for one of two reasons:</p>
<ul class="govuk-list govuk-list--bullet">
<li>an internal review is needed</li>
<li>further information from the applicant or Probate practitioner is needed</li>
</ul>
<p class="govuk-body-s">You will be notified by email if we need any information from you to progress the case.</p>
<p class="govuk-body-s">You'll usually get the grant within 16 weeks. It can take longer if you need to provide additional information.</p>
<p class="govuk-body-s">You don't need to do anything else now, we'll email you if we need more information</p>
<p class="govuk-body-s">Cafodd yr achos ei atal ar 26 Medi 2024 am un o ddau reswm:</p>
<ul class="govuk-list govuk-list--bullet">
<li>Mae angen adolygiad mewnol</li>
<li>Mae angen rhagor o wybodaeth gan y ceisydd neu'r ymarferydd profiant</li>
</ul>
<p class="govuk-body-s">Fe'ch hysbysir drwy e-bost os bydd arnom angen unrhyw wybodaeth gennych i symud yr achos yn ei flaen.</p>
<p class="govuk-body-s">Byddwch fel arfer yn cael y grant o fewn 16 wythnos. Gall gymryd mwy o amser os oes angen i chi ddarparu gwybodaeth ychwanegol.</p>
<p class="govuk-body-s">Nid oes angen i chi wneud unrhyw beth arall nawr, byddwn yn anfon e-bost atoch os oes arnom angen mwy o wybodaeth.</p>
